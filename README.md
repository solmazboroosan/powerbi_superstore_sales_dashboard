# 📊 Superstore Sales Dashboard | Power BI Project

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard created using the Superstore Sales Dataset.  
It provides a complete overview of sales performance, customer behavior, product categories, and regional trends.

The dashboard is designed in a clean executive style to help users quickly understand business performance and key insights.

---

## 🎯 Project Goals

- Monitor total sales and business growth
- Analyze customer and order activity
- Compare product category performance
- Evaluate sales by region
- Understand customer segments
- Build a professional business dashboard

---

## 📂 Dataset Source

Source: Kaggle - Superstore Sales Dataset

The dataset includes:

- Order ID
- Order Date
- Customer Name
- Product Name
- Category
- Region
- Segment
- Sales

---

## 📈 KPI Cards

### 🔹 Total Products
Displays the total number of products.

### 🔹 Total Customers
Shows the number of unique customers.

### 🔹 Total Orders
Displays total orders placed.

### 🔹 Average Sales
Average sales value per transaction.

### 🔹 Total Sales
Total revenue generated from all sales.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Visuals

### 📈 Sales by Year

Line chart showing sales growth over time.

Insight:  
Sales increased steadily from 2016 to 2018.

---

### 🍩 Sales by Category

Donut chart comparing:

- Technology
- Furniture
- Office Supplies

Insight:  
Technology has the highest sales contribution.

---

### 📍 Sales by Region

Bar chart comparing:

- West
- East
- Central
- South

Insight:  
West region generated the highest revenue.

---

### 👥 Sales by Segment

Column chart showing:

- Consumer
- Corporate
- Home Office

Insight:  
Consumer segment is the strongest customer group.
